Harnessing nature’s toolkit with uncompromising safety and quality

We take postnatal tissues, like Wharton’s Jelly, amnion, and umbilical cord blood, and turn them into biologics designed for homologous use.

Think of it as nature’s toolkit, with components like mesenchymal stem cells and cytokines that support the body’s natural processes.

But we don’t stop at sourcing. We’re serious about safety and quality. Every tissue donation goes through rigorous screening—because we’re committed to delivering products that healthcare providers can trust. From the moment tissues are collected to the point they’re cryopreserved, we follow strict protocols to make sure our biologics meet the highest standards of purity and bioactivity.

Let’s be clear: we don’t make therapeutic claims or promise miracle cures. Our focus is on producing high-quality, minimally manipulated biologics that are made for homologous use, just like the FDA requires. We’re passionate about what we do, and we're all about advancing science while playing by the rules.
